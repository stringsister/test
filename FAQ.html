How does the music selection work / how many songs should / can we pick?

Special requests are songs currently not on our repertoire. We accept two special requests songs as a part of the package price.

If you are hiring our services for the ceremony, we recommend you choose what you would like us to play for all the apply:

Bridal Party entrance
Groom entrance
Bride entrance
Signing of the registrar
Bride and Groom recessional

This is a basic template and can be adjusted for your personalized ceremony needs.

How do I Book The StringSisters?

We make a customized quote and contract for each event we book. In order to book our services, a $250 deposit must be sent to payments@stringsisters.ca and all documents must be signed. Then we are all set!

Can the harp and cello be moved from one location to another (example, from ceremony to cocktail hour location)?

Yes! We are able to set up and perform at one location and then relocate when needed. It is usually a 15-minute process (depending on the distance between two spots) to relocate and quickly re-tune our instruments after they have been packed up and unloaded.

Do musicians come with AV set up? Do musicians need to be mic’d?

At this time, we are a completely acoustic duo and do not offer any AV support. However, we are in the works to have a sound system for 2025 events.

We do not necessarily require to be mic’d, but we always recommend it when possible. Ceremonies do not require us to be mic’d but for a louder setting like a cocktail hour or reception, we recommend being mic’d.
